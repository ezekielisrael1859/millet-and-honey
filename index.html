Our Story

Baked with patience, shared with love

Millet & Honey started with one starter, fed on a kitchen counter, and loaves given to neighbors before there was ever a bakery. That's still how every loaf is made — one batch, one baker, no shortcuts added just because the bakery got bigger.

Today we still bake in small batches, every loaf scored by hand, every cake decorated one at a time. It's slower than most bakeries. We think that's exactly why it tastes better.
