Our Story

Baked with patience, shared with love

Millet & Honey began in a small Jos kitchen, with one starter and a stubborn belief that bread should never be rushed. What started as loaves for neighbors grew into a bakery built on the same principle — slow fermentation, honest ingredients, and a lot of hand-kneading.

Today we still bake in small batches, every loaf scored by hand, every cake decorated one at a time. It's slower than most bakeries. We think that's exactly why it tastes better.
